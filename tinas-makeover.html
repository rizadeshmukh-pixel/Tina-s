Our Story

Craft Honed
in the Heart of Dubai

Tina's MakeOver was founded with a singular vision — to bring the precision, technique, and luxury of international beauty artistry to the heart of South Gujarat.

Our founder trained and worked in Dubai's elite beauty circuit, absorbing the exacting standards demanded by clientele from over 200 nationalities. Every cut, contour, and colour treatment at Tina's carries that global perspective.
